Key Insights
Perris: Fastest-Growing, Least Saturated
Leading population growth at +0.60% YoY with the lowest business density in the cohort. Strong first-mover opportunity for food, daily services, and neighborhood retail.
Corona: Highest Consumer Spending Power
Median household income of $106,438, which is 16% above California's average. The strongest market for premium services, specialty retail, and higher price-point businesses.
All 5 Cities Are Regional Retail Anchors
Every city draws more shoppers than its local population alone would generate. Moreno Valley is the most balanced (-28.4) and has the most room for new locally-focused businesses.
